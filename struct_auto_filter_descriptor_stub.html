Constructs a new packet subscriber entry based on the specified call extractor and call pointer

The caller is responsible for decomposing the desired routine into the target AutoFilter call. The extractor is required to carry information about the type of the proper member function to be called; t_call is required to be instantiated by the caller and point to the AutoFilter proxy routine.
