9 /*
10  The auto_arg<T> classes are used to generate of auto_in and auto_out types
11  based on the argument T.
12  The core functionality is that all argument types are treated as standard
13  types, and can be re-derived from those standard types.
14  It is expected that every parent of auto_arg will define:
15  - id_type (an unqualified T)
16  - base_type (a T qualified by const and/or &)
17  - shared_type (the pointer T from which the parent inherits)
18  Furthermore, by classifying arguments in terms of orientation and fundamental
19  T, auto_arg enables the specification of a principal T to any class
20  implementing an AutoFilter method.
21  */
